=== CHATBOT FUNCTIONALITY ANALYSIS ===

## FUNCTIONALITIES (Workflow Structure)
- list_pizza_options: The chatbot provides a list of available pizza types
  - order_pizza: The chatbot assists users in placing orders for predefined or custom pizzas (Params: pizza type, pizza size)
    - custom_pizza_options: Users can customize their pizza by selecting specific ingredients (Params: ingredients)
    - multiple_pizza_orders: Users can place larger orders that include multiple pizzas and drinks (Params: pizza types, drink types)
- list_drink_options: The chatbot offers a list of available drinks
  - order_drinks: The chatbot helps users order drinks to accompany their pizzas (Params: drink type)
    - multiple_pizza_orders: Users can place larger orders that include multiple pizzas and drinks (Params: pizza types, drink types)
- opening_hours_information: The chatbot shares the operating hours of the service
- delivery_time_estimation: The chatbot provides an estimate of how long it takes to deliver an order
- crust_options_information: The chatbot informs users about various crust options for pizzas
- sauce_options_information: The chatbot provides details on different sauces available for pizzas
- special_menu_items: The chatbot informs users about unique or special items available on the menu
