=== CHATBOT FUNCTIONALITY ANALYSIS ===

## FUNCTIONALITIES (Workflow Structure)
- list_main_functions: The chatbot lists its primary services which include photography, video, and virtual/3D rendering.
  - offer_appointment_setup: The chatbot indicates its capability to set an appointment for a session.
    - set_appointment: The chatbot assists the user in scheduling an appointment by requesting necessary details such as name, phone number, and desired appointment date. (Params: name, phone_number, appointment_date, email_address)
      - confirm_appointment: The chatbot confirms the appointment details with the user after receiving the necessary information. (Params: name, appointment_date)
        - finalize_appointment: The user confirms the details of the appointment to finalize it for the specified date. (Params: appointment_confirmed)
    - discuss_additional_costs: The chatbot informs the user about potential additional costs for extra revisions or services not included in the standard package.
  - address_shop_issues: The chatbot is available to answer questions or provide assistance regarding issues related to the shop.
- provide_price_estimate: The chatbot provides an estimated price for the service based on the specified media and number of artworks, while noting that the estimate could vary based on additional factors. (Params: media, number_of_artworks)
  - request_price_estimate: The chatbot asks for additional details required to provide a price estimate for a photography session. (Params: media_type, number_of_artworks, type_of_artworks)
  - explain_price_inclusions: The chatbot describes what is included in the provided price estimate for the video project to help the user understand the services covered.
- provide_opening_hours: The chatbot provides information regarding the operational hours of the service.
- inquire_services_available: The user asks for information regarding the services offered in terms of artwork sessions.
- clarify_price_factors: The chatbot informs the user that several factors may influence the final price of the services but does not provide specifics on those factors.
- describe_portfolio: The chatbot provides examples of completed projects to illustrate the company's portfolio.
- provide_opening_hours_location_portfolio_pricing_structure: The chatbot offers to provide information regarding opening hours, location, portfolio, pricing structure, and types of media handled, despite not answering the user's initial question.
- inquire_media_types: The user asks about the types of media handled in photography services to gain a better understanding of the offerings.
- inquire_video_project_services: The user requests predefined services or packages available for video projects.
