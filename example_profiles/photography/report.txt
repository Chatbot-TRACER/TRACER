=== CHATBOT FUNCTIONALITY ANALYSIS ===

## FUNCTIONALITIES (Workflow Structure)
- appointment_scheduling: The chatbot assists users in setting up appointments for photo sessions (Params: name, phone number, email, date)
  - appointment_follow_up: The chatbot may follow up after the appointment scheduling process to confirm or finalize details
- price_estimates_provision: The chatbot provides tailored price estimates based on user input regarding artwork media and project specifics (Params: type of media, number of artworks, type of artworks)
  - price_estimate_follow_up: The chatbot may follow up after providing price estimates to clarify or confirm details related to the quote
- information_querying: The chatbot answers questions related to shop services, opening hours, and location
- customization_of_services: The chatbot allows clients to customize services or packages based on their specific needs and preferences (Params: media type, number of artworks, type of artworks)
  - customization_follow_up: The chatbot may follow up with users to confirm customizations and preferences regarding services
- request_for_special_features: Clients can inquire about special features available for their artworks and projects
- multimedia_inquiry: The chatbot facilitates inquiries about multiple media types simultaneously (Params: details for each media type)
