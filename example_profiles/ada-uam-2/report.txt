=== CHATBOT FUNCTIONALITY ANALYSIS ===

## FUNCTIONALITIES (Workflow Structure)
- list_main_capabilities: The chatbot provides a link to the services offered by the Tecnologías de la Información department.
- provide_contact_information: The chatbot provides multiple ways to contact the Centro de Atención a Usuarios, including email, phone, and physical address.
- provide_service_hours: The chatbot provides the operating hours for contacting the Centro de Atención a Usuarios.
- suggest_ticket_creation: The chatbot advises the user to open a ticket by emailing the CAU for specific technical assistance.
- provide_catalog_link: The chatbot offers the link to the catalog of services provided by the Centro de Atención a Usuarios.
- prompt_for_reformulation: The chatbot asks the user to reformulate their question for better understanding or suggests consulting the catalog of services instead.
- provide_service_catalog_link: The chatbot provides a link to the catalog of services offered by the Centro de Atención a Usuarios.
