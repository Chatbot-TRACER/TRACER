=== CHATBOT FUNCTIONALITY ANALYSIS ===

## FUNCTIONALITIES (Workflow Structure)
- provide_pizza_options: The chatbot provides a list of predefined pizza options available for ordering.
  - select_pizza_size: The user selects the size of the pizza they want to order. (Params: pizza_size)
    - confirm_order: The chatbot confirms the user's pizza order, including the size and type. (Params: pizza_type, pizza_size)
      - request_drink_options: The chatbot provides the available drink options for the user to choose from.
        - select_drink_order: The user selects the types and quantity of drinks they wish to order. (Params: drink_type, drink_quantity)
          - confirm_full_order: The chatbot summarizes the user's complete order, including the pizza and drinks, and provides the total cost and pickup details. (Params: pizza_type, pizza_size, drink_type, drink_quantity, total_cost, pickup_time, pickup_location, order_id)
            - provide_order_tracking_info: The chatbot explains the process for order pickup, indicating that there is no delivery service available.
      - confirm_full_order: The chatbot summarizes the user's complete order, including the pizza and drinks, and provides the total cost and pickup details. (Params: pizza_type, pizza_size, drink_type, drink_quantity, total_cost, pickup_time, pickup_location, order_id)
        - provide_order_tracking_info: The chatbot explains the process for order pickup, indicating that there is no delivery service available.
- provide_special_deals_info: The chatbot shares pricing details for pizzas, including any special deals or promotions.
