=== CHATBOT FUNCTIONALITY ANALYSIS ===

## FUNCTIONALITIES (Workflow Structure)
- welcome_user: The chatbot welcomes the user to the Pizza service and introduces its capabilities.
  - provide_pizza_options: The chatbot provides a list of available pizza types to order.
    - prompt_custom_pizza_options: The chatbot asks the user whether they would like to order a predefined pizza or a custom pizza.
      - provide_pizza_description: The chatbot provides a detailed description of the selected pizza, such as the margarita pizza. (Params: pizza_type)
        - confirm_pizza_order: The chatbot confirms the size of the pizza that the user has selected for the order. (Params: pizza_type, size)
          - prompt_drink_order: The chatbot prompts the user to select drinks after finalizing the pizza order.
            - confirm_drink_order: The chatbot confirms the details of the drink order, including the quantity and types. (Params: drink_type, quantity)
              - provide_order_summary: The chatbot provides a summary of the entire order, including the total cost and estimated readiness time. (Params: pizza_type, pizza_size, drink_order_summary, total_cost, preparation_time, pickup_location, order_id)
                - provide_pickup_instructions: The chatbot informs the user about the pickup policy and location for their order. (Params: location)
          - provide_order_summary: The chatbot provides a summary of the entire order, including the total cost and estimated readiness time. (Params: pizza_type, pizza_size, drink_order_summary, total_cost, preparation_time, pickup_location, order_id)
            - provide_pickup_instructions: The chatbot informs the user about the pickup policy and location for their order. (Params: location)
- provide_drink_options: The chatbot lists available drink options and their prices.
