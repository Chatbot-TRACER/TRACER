=== CHATBOT FUNCTIONALITY ANALYSIS ===

## FUNCTIONALITIES (Workflow Structure)
- get_user_identification_help: Provides users with guidance on how to obtain their ID-UAM identifier or reset their password, including links and steps to follow.
  - contact_cau: Allows users to contact the Centro de Atención a Usuarios (CAU) for assistance with their ID-UAM or account-related issues.
  - provide_password_reset_steps: Offers detailed steps on how to reset the user's password, including how to obtain an activation code.
    - provide_activation_code_instructions: Guides users on how to obtain the activation code necessary for changing their password.
  - fetch_identification_by_dni: Guides users on how to search for their ID-UAM identifier using their DNI (National ID).
  - modify_user_id: Informs users on how to request a modification of their ID-UAM identifier if they have changed their name or if it is not recognized.
- provide_cau_contact_info: Supplies users with detailed contact information for the Centro de Atención a Usuarios (CAU), including email, phone number, and physical address.
- offer_service_catalog: Directs users to the catalog of services offered by the CAU, providing a link for further information.
- provide_password_change_reminder: Reminds users to change their password periodically to avoid account locking.
