=== CHATBOT FUNCTIONALITY ANALYSIS ===

## FUNCTIONALITIES (Workflow Structure)
- provide_service_information: The chatbot provides relevant information about the services offered by Tecnologías de la Información at UAM, including contact details and service catalog.
- provide_contact_methods: The chatbot supplies various ways to contact the Centro de Atención a Usuarios (CAU), including email, phone, and physical address.
- share_campus_map_link: The chatbot offers a link to view a campus map for easier navigation to in-person service locations.
- specify_service_hours: The chatbot communicates the operational hours for the CAU, detailing both regular and reduced hours.
- prompt_for_topic_exploration: The chatbot invites the user to inquire about other related topics in Tecnologías de la Información.
- clarify_chatbot_role: The chatbot reiterates its purpose, reminding the user that it is available to assist with questions regarding CAU services.
- offer_information_access: The chatbot offers information access regarding the capabilities of the Center for User Attention in order to assist the user.
- suggest_service_catalog: The chatbot encourages users to consult the catalog of services offered by the CAU for more detailed information.
