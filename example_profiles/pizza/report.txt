=== CHATBOT FUNCTIONALITY ANALYSIS ===

## FUNCTIONALITIES (Workflow Structure)
- order_custom_pizza: Assists users in ordering a custom pizza by collecting size and toppings information. (Params: size, toppings)
  - calculate_price: Calculates the total price for a pizza and drink order. (Params: size, topping, drink_type, quantity)
  - provide_order_details: Provides the user with details of their order, including price and estimated pickup time.
- provide_pizza_options: Provides users with available types of pizzas to order.
  - create_custom_pizza: Allows users to select and mix different toppings for a custom pizza (Params: toppings (list of chosen ingredients))
- provide_pizza_sizes: Provides users with the available sizes for pizzas.
- order_drinks: Assists users in adding drink orders to their pizza order by collecting the type and quantity of drinks. (Params: drink_type, quantity)
