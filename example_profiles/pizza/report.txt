=== CHATBOT FUNCTIONALITY ANALYSIS ===

## FUNCTIONALITIES (Workflow Structure)
- provide_menu_items: Lists the types of pizzas available for order.
  - order_predefined_pizza: Helps users in ordering predefined pizza types available in the menu. (Params: pizza_type)
    - order_drinks: Assists users in selecting and ordering drinks to accompany their pizza order. (Params: drink_type)
  - order_custom_pizza: Assists users in creating and ordering a custom pizza based on their preferred toppings and size. (Params: toppings, size)
    - order_drinks: Assists users in selecting and ordering drinks to accompany their pizza order. (Params: drink_type)
- provide_price_info: Provides users with price information for pizzas and beverages.
- provide_order_information: Provides information on how to get the order and estimated time to receive it.
- provide_order_time: Provides users with an estimated time for their order preparation and delivery.
- check_opening_hours: Assists users in finding out the opening hours of the pizza establishment.
- provide_opening_hours: Provides the opening hours of the shop.
