=== CHATBOT FUNCTIONALITY ANALYSIS ===

## FUNCTIONALITIES (Workflow Structure)
- list_main_functions: The chatbot provides a brief overview of its main functions related to ordering pizzas and drinks.
  - provide_predefined_pizza_options: The chatbot lists the predefined pizza options available for ordering.
    - collect_pizza_size: The chatbot prompts the user to specify the size of the pizza they would like to order. (Params: pizza_type)
      - confirm_pizza_order: The chatbot confirms the user's selection of pizza type and size. (Params: pizza_type, pizza_size)
        - collect_drink_quantity: The chatbot asks the user how many drinks they would like to order. (Params: pizza_type, pizza_size)
          - provide_drink_options: The chatbot lists the drink options available for the user to choose from.
            - collect_drink_selection: The chatbot prompts the user to specify their drink choices after confirming the quantity. (Params: drink_quantity)
              - provide_order_summary: The chatbot provides a summary of the user's order, including total cost, preparation time, pickup location, and order ID. (Params: pizza_size, additional_items, total_cost, preparation_time, pickup_location, order_id)
- list_main_capabilities: The chatbot lists the types of information or assistance it can provide related to pizza orders.
- prompt_for_drink_selection: The chatbot prompts the user to specify the type of drink they would like to order.
